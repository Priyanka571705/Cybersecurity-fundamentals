# Common Ports and Protocols

## Theory
Ports allow services to communicate over a network.

## Common Ports
- 80 → HTTP
- 443 → HTTPS
- 22 → SSH
- 21 → FTP

## Basic Practical
Command:
netstat -an

Observation:
Shows active ports and connections.

## SOC Perspective
Unexpected open ports may indicate
unauthorized services or suspicious activity.
