# What is SOC?

## Simple Definition
SOC (Security Operations Center) is a team that continuously
monitors systems and networks to detect and respond to security threats.

## Why SOC is Important
- Detects security incidents
- Protects systems and data
- Responds to attacks quickly

## Simple Example
If a suspicious login happens at midnight,
SOC analysts investigate and take action.

## Note
SOC works 24/7 to keep systems secure.
